GT8 Ultra High Speed Charger

  The GT8 by CoreSWX captures the sleek and powerful design essence of high-end cars like Ferraris. Serving as Core's fastest charger, it emphasizes speed and efficiency with a stylish black exterior, a clean white interior, and a distinctive green LED underglow. The sheet metal construction ensures cost-effectiveness in manufacturing. Drawing inspiration from Ferrari's iconic style, the vibrant green LED not only adds a touch of flair but also distinguishes the GT8 in the charging landscape. This reliability extends to cinematographers, filmmakers, and grip specialists, offering seamless power for uninterrupted creativity on every project-a commitment reflected in its distinctive appearance.
