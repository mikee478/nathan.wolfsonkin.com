AlphaV Camera Power Supply

  The AlphaV was designed with a focus on silent operation for film sets, eliminating the need for a fan and emphasizing efficient ventilation. Incorporating a rope handle for easy transport, the securing parts were meticulously crafted to fit snugly within the tight quarters of the frame. This design approach resulted in a compact, fanless system with optimal heat management and a seamlessly integrated rope handle, catering to the specific demands of on-set practicality.
