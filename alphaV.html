AlphaV Camera Power Supply

  I designed the AlphaV with a few key priorities in mind. First and foremost, the unit had to operate silently, which meant forgoing a fan to adhere to noise limitations. This required incorporating ample ventilation to prevent overheating. Additionally, the housing had to strike a balance between a sleek, durable appearance and effective protection for the internal components. To ensure convenient transport, I included a rope handle which required its own set of internal parts for mounting. These parts were designed to fit snugly within the tight confines of the frame. The end result is a compact, fanless system with optimal heat management and a seamlessly integrated rope handle, tailored to meet the specific demands of on-set practicality.
