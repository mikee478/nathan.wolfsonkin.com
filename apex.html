Apex 360W Battery Pack

  The Apex High Voltage battery pack is purpose-built for high-power LED lighting, featuring a durable, user-friendly, and ergonomically designed enclosure. The injection-molded front shell seamlessly matches the pre-existing rear, resulting in a clean and robust overall design. This front shell's thoughtful design ensures visibility of indicator lights from all angles, enhancing user convenience. With a forward-looking approach and adaptability to evolving LED products, the design also incorporates safety measures to prevent accidental use on 14v equipment. The Apex seamlessly merges style and functionality in its ergonomic mechanical design, solidifying its position as the premier on-board power solution for professionals in the film industry.
