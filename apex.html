Apex 360W Battery Pack

  When I designed mechanicals of the Apex High Voltage battery pack, my emphasis was on a robust, sleek shell with a user-friendly design that complements its powerful internals. The injection-molded front seamlessly aligns with the pre-existing rear, achieving a clean, sturdy overall look. Notably, the design ensures the LED indicator lights are viewable from all angles, enhancing user convenience. Striking a balance between a sleek appearance and effective internal protection was crucial. The Apex battery pack is the result of this approach, offering style and functionality tailored to the specific demands of film industry professionals.
