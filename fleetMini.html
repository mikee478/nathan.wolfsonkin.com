Fleet Mini Quad Charger

  CoreSWX's Fleet Q4 Mini Charger features a streamlined mechanical design, emphasizing efficiency in charging. The compact platform accommodates four charging docks with ample clearance for Core's largest batteries, including the Apex. The top cover ingeniously supports both B-Mount and V-Mount charging docks, adding versatility to the design. The use of simple sheet metal ensures cost-effectiveness and easy manufacturing. Despite its small size, the charger maintains durability with a slim, lightweight aluminum build. The low-profile design optimizes space, making it a practical choice for various professional applications. The Fleet Q4 Mini Charger strikes a balanced combination of compactness, efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and durability, catering to the diverse needs of professionals in the field.
