Fleet Mini Quad Charger

  Designing the Fleet Q4 Mini Charger centered on key priorities. It efficiently accommodates four charging docks for Core's largest batteries, including the Apex. The top cover seamlessly supports both B-Mount and V-Mount charging docks. A simple sheet metal design ensures cost-effectiveness, and the slim aluminum build maintains durability. The low-profile design optimizes space for practical use. In essence, the Fleet Q4 Mini Charger is a compact, versatile solution, balancing efficiency and durability at a reasonable cost.
