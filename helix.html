Helix Max Battery Pack

  I designed the Helix Max with key priorities in mind. The goal was to achieve a sleek and robust aesthetic reminiscent of the Apex. I incorporated a backlit runtime LCD screen, ensuring quick access to essential information such as estimated and actual runtime, charge status, and remaining capacity. Taking ergonomic considerations into account, I aimed to provide a comfortable and user-friendly experience for industry professionals. The wrap-around LED gauge and rugged polycarbonate housing were meticulously crafted to enhance the Helix Max's identity as a reliable, high-performance power source. This mechanical design seamlessly combines convenience, ergonomic elements, and durability, meeting the specific demands of professionals in the cinema industry.
