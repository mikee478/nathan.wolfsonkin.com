Mach4 Dual Charger

  The Mach4 Dual's standout feature is its slim design, over .5" thinner than comparable models. Specifically designed to perfectly accommodate two of CoreSWX's largest batteries, the Apex, it features two independent charge bays for simultaneous charging. With a robust aluminum build ensuring durability, the mechanical design prioritizes practicality, making the Mach4 Dual efficient and reliable for battery charging.
