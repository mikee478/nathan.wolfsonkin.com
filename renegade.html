Renegade Power Station

  The Renegade Power Station is designed with a focus on efficient manufacturing. Tailored for the injection molding process, it adopts the sleek aesthetic of the Maverick block battery. A single mold is used to create both halves of the battery, streamlining production. Notably, the Renegade's polycarbonate outer housing shares the Maverick's size, seamlessly fitting into legacy shipping cases for added convenience. This design approach ensures a straightforward manufacturing process while providing compatibility with existing equipment, making the Renegade a practical and convenient choice for users.
