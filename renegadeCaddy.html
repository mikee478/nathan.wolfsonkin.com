Power Station Caddy

  The Power Station Caddy features a straightforward, robust, and cost-effective design tailored for sheet metal fabrication. Compatible with Core's Renegade, Renegade XL, and Maverick block batteries, it offers elevated storage, ensuring the easy movement of wheeled light stands with added stability, even when supporting the extra weight of the battery pack. Its mechanicals embody simplicity, making it an efficient and reliable accessory for professionals.
